Supplies

1. Television Camera

Supplies is VERY important while stealing signs in baseball the first thing you will need is a television camera shown below

Cameras like these must be hidden from the crowd to see. These cameras can zoom in up to 400 feet! The average cost of one camera can cost in a range of 5000-10000 dollars depending on the quality.

2. Trash Can

Any trash can will do but this object will be used when you are trying to give a signal to the batter. This will be explained more in Step 2.

3. Baseball Bat

Any baseball bat will do but this bat will also be used to give a signal to the batter. The bat will hit the trash can as the sign from the catcher is read.
