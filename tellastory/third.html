What happens when your caught?

The MLB can give a limited amount of punishments that can effect the team in the future. Here is a list of some punishments that could be given or have been given to other teams.

List of possible consequences

  1. Suspending of coaches and managers
  2. A fine of a maximum 5 million dollars
  3. Loss of first and second round draft picks. (Prospects that are not in the MLB)
