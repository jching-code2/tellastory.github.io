What is sign stealing?

In baseball, sign stealing is a method where the team that is batting figures out what pitch the pitcher will throw before. For instance, the catcher will show a certain number of fingers to the pitcher (indicating what pitch they want the pitcher to throw). If batters are able to figure out these signs before the pitcher throws, they will be put at a higher advantage to get a hit.

Has anyone been caught?

Recently, the Houston Astros have been caught stealing signs all the way back in the 2017 season. Every person that has been involved (besides players) in this scandal have been fired. Here is a table that shows all the people who have been fired.
